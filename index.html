Manga Genres

Manga, the captivating world of Japanese comics covers a variety of genres which not only help its readers to grasp better understanding of the entire story but also provides deep insights about the Japanese culture. Let’s embark our journey to explore the famous manga genres!

Shonen:

Shonen manga targets the young male audience covering a combination of action-packed adventures and courageous stories along with demonstration of martial arts skills. Popular manga series include “Dragon Ball” & “Naruto” exemplify the Shonen manga, captivating readers with vibrant characters.

Shojo:

Shojo manga targets especially teenage female audience covering a mixture of romance, friendship and relationships. These stories revolve around young heroines encompassing love, romance with strong emphasis on emotions and character development. Famous manga series include “Sailor Moon” & Fruits Basket” captured the hearts of readers through compelling story art.

Seinen:

Seinen manga targets adult male readers and revolves around mature characters, themes including politics and psychology. These stories include complex characters and put light into the darker aspects of human nature and society. Famous manga series include “Monster” & Berserk” offer diversified range of storytelling work to the target audience.

Yaoi and Yuri:

Yaoi and yuri manga depicts romance and sexual relationships between the characters of same gender. Yaoi focuses on male-male relationships while yuri focuses on female-female relationships. Famous manga series include in Yaoi genre is “Jinx Manga” and in Yuri series is “Yuri!!! On Ice”.
